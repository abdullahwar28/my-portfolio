Latest Project

TrackMate

TrackMate is a cross-platform mobile app developed to support hikers on the Bibbulmun Track in Western Australia. It includes features such as QR code scanning, geolocation, camera integration, offline data storage, and local notifications. As part of the pathlabs team at Murdoch University Dubai, I contributed to the development of key modules including Issue Reporting, Survey, and QR scanning, focusing on offline functionality and user interaction.
